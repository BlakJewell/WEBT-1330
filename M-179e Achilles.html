The Achilles is the most powerful precision rifle available on the market. Each round fired with the Achilles will split up into a pentagon pattern comprised of 5 individual projectiles. When charged, the weapon will concentrate the projectiles in a center spot, inflicting maximum damage to anything or anyone hit, even through whatever cover or object may be in front of them. The weapon has a maximum clip size of 9 rounds. It does not have a scope slot and instead comes equipped with holographic iron sights by default. Due to the Tech mechanism in place, it is not possible to install a Muzzle.
